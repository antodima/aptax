Audiosource is a Web audio / video mixer for Youtube, Soundcloud and Jamendo.

You can also create and share your music with sequencer and modular synths.

Be creative in your Live sessions and Sessions you can play also songs, you can use the pads to play your jingle.

You can also control all decks and mixer via midi.

check for the [?] help buttons in each page to read the step by step guide.

do not forget to open the mini controller on the rec bar and play...

Have fun!

Known Bugs or Dysfunctions
