We always have a variety of lovable cats and dogs available for adoption. Kittens are often available and puppies and small animals are sometimes available. Visit one of our galleries above to find an animal ready for adoption!

Adoption Center Hours

Monday - Saturday: noon - 5 p.m.

Wednesday: noon - 6 p.m.

Sunday: closed

Adoption Fees



Dogs - $100. Dogs are spayed/neutered, up-to-date on vaccinations, microchipped, and tested for heartworm disease.

Puppies (less than 4 months)- $125. Puppies are spayed/neutered, up-to-date on vaccinations for their age, and microchipped.

Cats - $50. Cats are spayed/neutered, up-to-date on vaccinations, microchipped, and tested for FIV/FELV.

Kittens (less than 4 months) - $75. Kittens are spayed/neutered, up-to-date on vaccinations for their age, microchipped, and tested for FIV/FELV.

Small animals - fees vary. Please contact us for more information.
