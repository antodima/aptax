Are you a Maple champion? Set world records as you play by performing goals like defeating the highest numbers of monsters, dying the most times, picking up the most items, and more. Keep playing to surpass the current record and set a new one!

Use the Maple World Records Book to keep track of who holds the current records in your world and see what you need to do to beat them! Some records are hidden and you have to solve hints to find out what they are.

Every time you become a record holder, you’ll receive rewards. Earn a special title to show that you’re the current World Record holder. As you keep collecting records, you’ll earn restorative items, Spell Traces, EXP and Drop coupons, effects, potential items, and more!
