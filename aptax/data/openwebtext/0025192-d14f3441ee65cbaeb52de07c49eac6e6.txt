Justin Braun never got regained his starting position as soon as Bernardo Corradi came in to the club and even more when Marco Di Vaio was signed as a Designated Player. He is a hard working player but the fit between the role he was filling and what he could bring technically quickly became a gap. It was a question of time for him to be traded especially that it helps the Montreal Impact in its salary cap situation.
