Lately it seems like anybody and everybody has been creating nail polish collections inspired by today's hit TV shows, and now another one is about to join the mix. Lena Dunham, the creator, director and star of HBO's series "Girls" has teamed up with Deborah Lippmann to make this collection possible.





Next week, 4 unique shades will become available, each color representing one of the hit characters of the show: Hannah, Marnie, Shoshanna, and Jessa. Deborah Lippmann was kind enough to give us a sneak peek of these shades on her website.

Hannah is a described as a "hopeless hunter green" and appears to be pale green shade with little to no shimmer. Marnie, described as a "prim and proper pink" appears to be just that. Light pastel pink perfect for those upcoming Spring days. Shoshanna, described as a"virtuous vivid violet" is another soft pastel in a purple shade and Jessa appears to be the perfect "Bohemian burgundy" to round the collection out.





The collection will become available next week and will retail for $45.00.
