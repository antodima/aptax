Reacting to the deteriorating and order situation in Uttar Pradesh, (BJP) leader Subramanian Swamy on Monday said that Article 256 empowers the government to give directions to a state government, and that even President's rule can be imposed on Uttar Pradesh, if they refused to follow those directions.

"Articles 256 empowers the central government to give directions to state government in such a situation, and if they refuse to do that, Presidents rule can be imposed on the state," Subramanian Swamy.
