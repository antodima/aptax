Three people from Blount County are facing felony reckless endangerment charges after deputies say they forced a minor to inject methamphetamine.

Deputies say 29-year-old Samuel Thomas Hill, 32-year-old Michael Eugene Hill and 18-year-old Felicia Faith Hannah lured a 17-year-old girl in the bedroom of Samuel Hill’s home at Butterfly Gap Loop Road on Tuesday, held her down by her arms and injected her with methamphetamine.

Investigators say the girl was injected with meth against her will. She later had bruising on her arms and tested positive for methamphetamine at a hospital.

Samuel and Michael Hill are being held on a $25,000 bond and have a court date on April 8th. A bond has not been set for Hannah at this time.

The incident is still under investigation.
